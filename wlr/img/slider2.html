KNOW US

We Love to Recycle is an initiative by the Earth Sense Recycle, an India's leading e-waste recycler that is our passionate about leaving the world better than we found it in whatever the way possible. The world's fast-depleting resources and the pressing need to conserve the precious little we still have were the wake-up call to do our bit for the environment. We call every one to rethink and recycle!!

We Love to Recycle is an endeavour that endorses our commitment to do what we love doing best: recycle, and to spread awareness among Indians that reside in schools, colleges, RWAs, Society, or any where in India. We mainly focus on the young minds, they are the ones in the future who have the capability to change our tomorrow, today.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to urge individuals and institutions to be awake, aware, alert, and to arise and act against the cause of e waste pollution, an issue that is not only relevant to our age and times but one that will impact the future of all our tomorrows. For conserving our current resources, the remedy is only recycling.

MANAGE WASTE

Here is your chance to finally kick-start those corporate social responsibility initiatives that you, as an organization, have always wanted to implement but didn't know how to go about it. Just lend a hand and all your e-waste (end-of-life electronic waste) to Earth Sense Recycle Pvt. Ltd., a company that is committed to the scientific and eco-friendly management of electronic waste. The company will provide you with bins at your campus, and all you have to do is dispose of the e-waste at your institution into these bins. Earth Sense will go on collection drives at regular intervals to pick up these bins, after which the e-waste will be recycled in its state-of-the-art factories.

WHY RECYCLE

For those of you who are wondering why all this fuss about recycling, maybe these facts will give you some insight...

  • 75% of produced waste can actually be recycled.
  • Plastic takes up to 500 years to decompose, glass 4,000 years, diskettes 500 years, and ink cartridges 1,000 years. Till then it will occupy our water bodies, soils etc.
  • Plastic bags and other plastic garbage thrown into the ocean kill as many as 1,000,000 sea creatures every year.
  • Recycling plastic saves twice as much energy as burning it in an incinerator.
  • If all our newspaper was recycled, we could save about 250,000,000 trees each year. Recycled paper produces 73% less air pollution than if it were made from raw materials.
